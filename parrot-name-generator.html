How to choose a parrot name

Say the name out loud, check that it does not sound like a command, and choose a name that matches your pet’s color, behavior, breed or personality.

FAQs

What should I name my pet?

Pick a name that is short, clear, positive and easy to call. Names with one or two syllables often work best.

What are unique pet names?

Unique pet names include Mochi, Nova, Clover, Atlas, Echo, Maple, Zuzu, Sage, Rio and Pixel.

Are short pet names better?

Short names are usually easier for pets to recognize and easier for owners to repeat during training.

Can I rename an adopted pet?

Yes. Use the new name with treats, praise and consistency for a few weeks.
